EN

"The most enjoyable way of learning is through games." Our vision in games is to provide relaxation and long-lasting learning through fun. In the final section, we created a Multidimensional Viewer next to the game to help users feel calmer. In total, we have 4 games.

How Are the Games Played?

Our first game is Space with Earth. In this game, the UFO aims to shoot fruits coming from Earth using the PgUp and PgDn keys, and the game starts with the "Start" button. Each fruit adds 100 points to the score, and the game ends if the UFO fails to catch a fruit and the atmosphere breaks apart.

Warning Incoming!

UFO: Help me before the explosive fruits destroy the atmosphere!

Our second game is Space Game. Unlike Space with Earth, bullets continuously fall toward the rocket. The rocket must escape alien astronauts using the HOME and END keys and destroy them by pressing the spacebar. If all aliens are destroyed, the game ends with victory, but if the rocket is hit three times, the game ends with defeat.
Alien Alert!

Rocket: Save me from the aliens so I can complete my journey!
Our third game is Space Maze. The goal is for the UFO to complete the given maze within fifteen seconds. If the maze is not completed within this time, the game ends.

Explosion Warning!

UFO: If I cannot find the exit within fifteen seconds, I will explode!

Our fourth game is Space Jump Go. The goal is for the rocket to reach infinity in space without shaking. As the shaking increases, the risk of falling becomes higher. The rocket progresses by jumping on supports, and as the game continues, the supports behind disappear. The game ends if the rocket falls into empty space. The score is displayed on the screen, and when it reaches two hundred and fifty, the game ends with victory and the rocket flies into infinity. Refreshing the page restarts the game.

Shaking Warning!

Rocket: If I shake while jumping on the supports, I will fall down!
